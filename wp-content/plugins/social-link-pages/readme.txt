== Description ==
The WordPress alternative to Linktree, About.me, Carrd.co, and more! Create mobile-friendly, link-in-bio landing pages with all your social media links for sharing with your followers and promoting yourself! Capture emails. Grow your audience. Drive traffic. Promote your new book, album, or product. All for free!

The Social Link Pages WordPress plugin in 60 seconds:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E9AH40tfteA

Check out **our** Social Link Page: [https://sociallinkpages.com/links](https://sociallinkpages.com/links)

Then get more out of your pages with the [Social Link Pages Pro](https://sociallinkpages.com/social-link-pages-pro/) add-on.

= Features =

* Create unlimited, targeted pages for all your social profiles
* Social Link Page urls use your own domain for better branding
* Create multiple landing pages, micro-landing pages, or squeeze pages for your art, album, poem, recipe, or whatever you create
* Social Link Pages are mobile-first, responsive, and look great on any device
* Customize your pages with links, colors, buttons, images, and more
* Edit your pages in seconds from your phone, tablet, or desktop
* In-depth analytics: See how many people viewed each page and which buttons they click on most
* Mailchimp sign-up integration
* Built-in contact form
* **GDPR-compliant** because all data is hosted on your server

All free! And if you want more, check out the [Pro version](https://sociallinkpages.com/social-link-pages-pro/).
